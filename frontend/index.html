QUESTIONS, ANSWERED

Understand your vault.

Everything important before you arm an autonomous recovery policy.

Can the AI release my assets by itself?＋

No. The LLM only classifies evidence. GhostVaultCore requires both a missed heartbeat and the full grace period before anyone can finalize a release.

What happens if Ritual precompiles are temporarily unavailable?＋

The Agent includes an owner-controlled bypass mode for testnet resilience. It is disabled on the live deployment and cannot bypass the Core contract's time conditions.

Can I stop a recovery after the grace period begins?＋

Yes, until the grace deadline. The vault owner or designated guardian can send a heartbeat to return the vault to Armed state.

Do I need to connect a wallet to view vaults?＋

No. The dashboard reads Ritual's public RPC immediately. A wallet is required only for actions that create or change on-chain state.
